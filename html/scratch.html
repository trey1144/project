Sampling in Hip Hop
Resume

Breaking down samples in songs:

  1. Origins
    • Hip hop emerged from the DJ culture in the Bronx, New York.
    • DJs like Kool Herc and Grandmaster Flash would loop drum breaks from funk and soul records to create extended dance sections.
  2. Sampling techniques
    • Sampling can involve taking a short drum break, a melodic riff, or even spoken words from an existing recording.
    • The sampled material is then manipulated using various techniques such as chopping, pitch-shifting, time-stretching, and filtering to create a new and unique sound.
